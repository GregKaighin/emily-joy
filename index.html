About Emily

Emily Joy

Professional Singer

Emily Joy is a warm, versatile professional singer based in Lincolnshire, performing jazz, pop and soul for weddings, corporate events and private functions across the region and beyond. With a naturally expressive voice and a genuine love of live performance, Emily brings both elegance and energy to every occasion.

From a heartfelt solo during a wedding ceremony to a lively evening set that gets guests on their feet, Emily reads the room, builds the energy and shapes every performance around the moment — leaving everyone wanting one more song.

Before You Book

Common Questions

What music styles do you cover?

Emily's repertoire spans jazz standards, soul, pop and musical theatre — equally at home with a Sinatra classic and a modern chart hit. Browse the repertoire section for a flavour of what she sings.

Do you perform with backing tracks or live music?

Emily performs with premium professional backing tracks as a soloist, or can also perform as part of the Emily & Greg Duo with live piano accompaniment.

How long do you perform for?

The typical format is 2 × 45-minute sets, but timings are always flexible — Emily is happy to tailor the schedule to fit your event perfectly.

Do you travel outside Lincolnshire?

Yes — while Emily is based in Lincolnshire and performs regularly across the county, she's happy to travel further afield for the right event. Get in touch and let's chat.
